Join Us - To join our group either register through the ourwatch website or email us direct at contact@westdidscommunity.co.uk.

Become a coordinator for your street - We are looking to recruit coordinators for certain streets in our area. Coordinators are responsible for ensuring that relevant information is passed on to people on their street who are not members of the Neighbourhood watch. If you fancy becoming a coordinator send us an email.

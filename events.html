Over 50s Coffee Afternoon

Starting from the 12th June, the over 50s coffee afternoon will take place every other Tuesday from 1:30-3:30 pm at Christ Church Hall. See the schedule below for a full list of dates.

We provide free tea, coffee and cake, as well as a variety of board games. We would like to provide special events too, anything from craft sessions to yoga. So if you have a suggestion for an event you think we should put on please let us know.

Next Coffee Afternoon Special Event

Pebble Painting - 4th September

We will be taking part in the artistic trend making waves not only in Manchester but all over the UK, pebble painting. We will be providing all art materials and all pebbles. Though if you want to go collecting and bring your own pebbles you are more than welcome to.

Other events

Cycle Tune-Up - 4th September 10:30- 12:00

Do you want to get out on your bike but it's looking a little worse for wear? Then come and join us for a cycle tune-up event, sponsored by Cycle Surgery, to get your bike back in shape. Come along for some free advice about how to look after your bike, and there is even a free bike maintenance workshop at 11:00 am. There will also be chains and inner tubes available to buy which will be fitted for free. The event is taking place on the Southway green space opposite to the entrance to the playing fields (click the poster to see a version with a map).

Estate Tidy-Up - 29th September 2pm

We're holding an estate tidy-up day, involving litter picking and weeding. We'll be trying to litter pick over as much of the estate as possible, as well as weeding on Mersey Crescent, Northbank Walk and Riverbank Walk. How much we can do depends on how many people come. We will provide all litter picking equipment, but if you have any weeding tools please bring them along.

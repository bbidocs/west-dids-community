Over 50s Coffee Afternoon

Starting from the 12th June, the over 50s coffee afternoon will take place every other Tuesday from 1:30-3:30 pm at Christ Church Hall. See the schedule below for a full list of dates.

We provide free tea, coffee and cake, as well as a variety of board games. We would like to provide special events too, anything from craft sessions to yoga. So if you have a suggestion for an event you think we should put on please let us know.

Next Coffee Afternoon Special Events

Christmas crafts - 27th November

I know it's not Christmas yet, but it's not too early to get ahead of the game on making some decorations. We'll be making ornate 3D snowflakes, and cardboard "gingerbread houses" with battery powered tea lights inside. All materials provided and you can take home everything you make.


Laughter Yoga - 11th December

Come and join us for a laughter yoga session. The idea is that all laughter is good for you, even if it is forced. And what usually starts with forced laughter, ends up being genuine hilarity. You can see people giving it a go on YouTube.
